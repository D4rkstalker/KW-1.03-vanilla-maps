Deperate Measures (Kane's Wrath mission map) 
By PurpleScrin

This is the first time I made a mission map with 3 mission objectives and it is a similar remake of a Nod mission from C&C1 Covert Operations titled "Under Siege C&C". I know the map is made basic because that's the way the original map was made without additional textures and objects but the scripts I made were customized and there are 3 difficulty settings.
