Map Name: Tiberium Stadium 2
Author Name: Predatore
Max. Players: 4
Game: C&C 3: Kane's Wrath
Type of Map: Skirmish
Description: This map has a stadium in the center, and a functional monorail circuit (it has 4 trains moving). The tiberium inside the stadium regenerated fast, and there are 8 tiberium spikes in it. Each player starts outside the stadium with a blue tiberium field near, and a tibeirum spike.
