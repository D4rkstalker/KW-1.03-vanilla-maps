Map Name: Tournament Patagonia
Author Name: Predatore
Max. Players: 4
Game: C&C 3: Kane's Wrath
Type of Map: Skirmish
Description: A blue zone, almost vertical symmetrical map. There are mountains on the left, and the ocean on the right. There is a lot of grass, and some farms and a factory. A monorail cross the map. There are 4 tiberium spikes, and some blue tiberium on the sides of the map. This map is intend to play on teams, locating "Upper Team" vs "Bottom Team".
