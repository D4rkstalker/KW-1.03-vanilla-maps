Map Name: Redzone Midnight
Author Name: Predatore
Max. Players: 2
Game: C&C 3: Kane's Wrath
Type of Map: Skirmish
Description: This is a redzone map at midnight. It is very dark, and some enemy units can be difficult to see until it is too late. There are also some massive amounts of green tiberium, which leads to an intense gameplay. There are six tiberium spikes at the center of the map, and two EMP control centers at the sides.
